How Questly Works

🎮 The Board

You play on a 3×3 board. Tap a square to reveal a question.

✅ Claiming Squares

Answer correctly → you claim the square with X.
Answer wrong or time runs out → the square is marked O.

🏆 Winning

Connect 3 X's in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win the day.

🛟 Lifelines

You get two lifelines each day:
50:50 — removes two wrong answers (costs 80 XP).
Skip Tile — skip the question (costs 120 XP). A new question replaces it and you can try the tile again.
Each lifeline can be used only once per day.

⏱️ Timer

You have 12 seconds per question. If time runs out, the square is marked O.

📅 Daily Play

You can play only once per day. Come back tomorrow to continue your streak and claim your daily XP.
